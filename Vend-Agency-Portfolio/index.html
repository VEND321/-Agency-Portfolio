Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started

What is SEO?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) helps your website appear higher in search results like Google. By optimizing keywords, content, and technical setup, you attract more visitors without paid ads.

Example for event planners: If you organize weddings, good SEO ensures your site ranks when couples search for "best wedding planners in [your city]."

How long does it take to build my website?

Most websites are completed within 7-14 days after we receive all your content (photos, text, etc.). More complex projects may take 3-4 weeks. We offer rush delivery for an additional fee.

Do I need to pay everything upfront?

No! We require 50% to start the project, and the remaining 50% only after you approve the final design. This ensures you're completely satisfied before making the final payment.

What if I need changes after launch?

We offer affordable maintenance plans starting at ₦10k/month for updates, security checks, and minor changes. For larger updates, we provide custom quotes based on the work required.

Will my website work on mobile phones?

Absolutely! All our websites are designed mobile-first, ensuring perfect display and functionality on all devices from smartphones to desktop computers.
